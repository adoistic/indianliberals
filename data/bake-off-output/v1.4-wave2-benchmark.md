3. **page_system flip handling**: 9 flips were all legitimate (epigraph/preface pages without folios). Working as designed.

4. **extent_caveat boundary**: 2 minor model errors at the 0.30 threshold (set true when actually 0.35 = above). Not blocking; consider validator post-correction.

5. **Gujarati OCR**: khoj-2007 + khoj-2009 surfaced 16 transcription_anomaly flags for Gujarati script OCR garbles. Pattern consistent — Gujarati OCR is noisier than Devanagari. Consider a flag for "language-OCR-quality" at the work level for downstream UI.
