The following booklet titled “Forum of Free Enterprise – Code of Conduct” commences by pledging its working activities towards generating public awareness of the significance of free enterprise. It’s codes are built on the edifice of values such as honesty, hard work, courtesy and continuous initiative. It holds producers accountable for the quality of goods sold, while ensuring that employers also promote the welfare of their workers through fair wages, pleasant working conditions and opportunities for skill-improvement. Establishment of democratic forums and trade unions should be encouraged to promote creative cooperation. On a similar note, management must also ensure a fair distribution of monetary resources while adhering to honest business practices. This involves rational monetary investments towards modernisation and research along with payment of wages. The forum condemns malpractices of black-marketing and profiteering and calls upon professionals to truthfully render their services. The overall obligations of the members is to promote social welfare without concentrated wealth accumulation.
