Rabindranath Tagore was a poet, writer, thinker and Indian Liberal. On Rabindra Jayanti, we revisit Streer Potro or A Wife’s Letter – an epistolary tale that talks about the individuality of a woman. Tagore’s women protagonists often challenged the norm, much like Streer Potro’s Mrinal.
